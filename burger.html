HeYBurger!!

Introducing our mouthwatering Classic Cheeseburger: a juicy, perfectly seasoned beef patty, grilled to perfection and topped with a layer of melted cheddar cheese. Nestled in a soft, toasted brioche bun, this burger is adorned with crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and tangy pickles, all drizzled with our signature burger sauce. Each bite delivers a harmonious blend of savory flavors and delightful textures, making it the ultimate comfort food experience. Whether you're craving a quick lunch or a satisfying dinner, our Classic Cheeseburger promises to hit the spot every time.

Ingredients
    • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend)
    • Salt and pepper
    • 4 slices cheddar cheese
    • 4 brioche buns
    • Lettuce leaves
    • 1 large tomato, sliced
    • Pickles, sliced
    • 1 small onion, thinly sliced (optional)
    • 4 tbsp burger sauce (or mayonnaise and ketchup mix)
    Reciepe
    1. Prepare the Patties
    2. Grill the Patties
    3. Melt the Cheese
    4. Toast the Buns
    5. Assemble the Burger
