Pizza

YumPizza

Indulge in the simplicity and elegance of our Classic Margherita Pizza, a timeless favorite that showcases the best of traditional Italian flavors. This pizza features a hand-tossed, golden-brown crust, perfectly crispy on the outside and tender on the inside. A rich layer of tangy tomato sauce is spread evenly over the dough, providing a robust foundation for the toppings. Generous amounts of creamy, melted mozzarella cheese blanket the sauce, creating a gooey, irresistible texture with every bite. Fresh basil leaves are added immediately after baking, infusing the pizza with their fragrant aroma and vibrant taste. A light drizzle of high-quality olive oil and a sprinkle of salt, pepper, and dried oregano complete this classic dish, enhancing its flavors and adding a touch of gourmet flair. Perfect for any occasion, our Classic Margherita Pizza is a celebration of simplicity and flavor, bringing a piece of Italy to your table.

Ingredients
  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tsp dried oregano (optional)
  • Cornmeal or flour (for dusting)
Receipe
  1. Preheat the Oven
  2. Prepare the Dough
  3. Add the Sauce
  4. Add the Cheese
  5. Bake the Pizza
  6. Add Fresh Basil
  7. Finish with Olive Oil and Seasoning
  8. Serve
